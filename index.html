Constellations

Exploring Artists’ Networks

Constellations visualizes the relationships between contemporary artists working in various fields – painting, drawing, sculpture, photography, film, installation, sound, music, writing, generative art, mixed media, and performance – creating an inside view into the networks of selected artists presented on visitmytent. Do the artists know each other, have they exhibited together or even collaborated together on a piece of art? Different vectors connect individuals depending on the manner of their relation. The depth of their relationship influences their proximity in the network. Selecting a specific artist points out connected artists as well as the related interview and studio visit.

The visualization was developed as a collaboration between Stephanie Neumann and Alsino Skowronnek in Berlin, 2019.
